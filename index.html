Tanks!

Derived from my passion for games, this applicaton is built using Javascript, jQuery, and CSS.

The goal of this game is to destroy the tank bots before they destroy you!

Controls to play:

  • W to move in the direction you are facing
  • A and D to turn left and right
  • Mouse to aim
  • Space to shoot
